
Here is a comprehensive design guide to translate this futuristic, synthwave vibe into your app's user interface.

## Color Palette

The core of this aesthetic is the extreme contrast between pitch-black shadows and vibrant, glowing neon lights. 

| Role | Color Name | Hex Code Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Background** | Void Black | `#000000` or `#05050A` |
| **Surface/Card Background** | Deep Cyber Blue | `#0A1128` or `#121A33` |
| **Primary Accent (Glow)** | Neon Crimson/Pink | `#FF0A54` or `#FA003F` |
| **Secondary Accent** | Electric Violet/Blue | `#4D00FF` or `#00E5FF` |
| **Primary Text** | Ice White | `#F4F6FF` |
| **Secondary Text** | Muted Cyan | `#8AB4F8` |

---

## Typography

To match the high-tech, futuristic armor worn by the character, your fonts should be geometric, sharp, and highly legible against dark backgrounds.

* **Headers & Titles:** Use a stylized, technical sans-serif font. Great Google Font options include **Orbitron**, **Rajdhani**, or **Syncopate**. Use these in bold or black weights.
* **Body Text:** Keep it clean and highly readable to balance the intense headers. Use wide, modern sans-serif fonts like **Inter**, **Montserrat**, or **Space Grotesk**.
* **Text Styling:** Avoid using pure white (`#FFFFFF`) for large blocks of text to prevent eye strain; use a slightly off-white or light cyan instead. 

---

## UI Components & Styling

The character's suit features smooth armor plating with glowing energy lines. You can replicate this in your UI elements.

* **Buttons:** Make primary calls-to-action stand out using your Neon Crimson accent. Add a soft, glowing drop shadow (e.g., `box-shadow: 0 0 10px #FF0A54`) to mimic the visor's light.
* **Cards and Containers:** Use the Deep Cyber Blue for card backgrounds. Give them a subtle, 1px bright border (either electric blue or hot pink) to simulate the glowing panel gaps on the armor.
* **Icons:** Use crisp, outline-style icons. Fill them with your secondary electric blue or neon pink to make them pop against the dark background. 
* **Gradients:** Use subtle gradients on active elements, transitioning from dark purple to neon pink, to mimic the lighting falloff on the character's muscles.
* **Dark Mode Depth:** Instead of heavy drop shadows (which get lost on black backgrounds), use lighter inner borders or subtle colored glows to separate overlapping elements.
